Build a sports discovery and registration platform for Saudi Arabia called "SportPass" (سبورت باس).

Tagline: "Your pass to sport in Saudi Arabia" / "بطاقتك للرياضة في المملكة"

It is inspired by minidrett.no in structure and UX — clean, card-based, professional — 
adapted for KSA, bilingual Arabic/English, with RTL support.

---

WHAT THE PLATFORM DOES

SportPass helps people in Saudi Arabia:
1. Discover sports available in their region
2. Find clubs for each sport near them
3. Browse and register for coaching courses & certifications
4. Purchase sport licenses

---

SITE STRUCTURE & PAGES

1. Home — hero, sport category grid, featured clubs, how it works section
2. Sports — browse all 5 sports with search/filter
3. Sport Detail — overview of one sport + tabs for: Clubs / Courses / Licenses
4. Club Detail — club info, location, contact, how to join
5. Courses — browse all available coaching courses across sports
6. Licenses — browse and purchase sport licenses per sport
7. Login / Register
8. User Dashboard — my memberships, my licenses, my courses, my sports

---

DESIGN — inspired by minidrett.no:

- Clean white background (#ffffff)
- Light gray section backgrounds (#f5f5f5) for alternating sections
- Primary color: deep green (#1a5c38) — Saudi national color
- Accent color: gold (#c8a84b)
- Font: Inter (English), Tajawal (Arabic) — both available on Google Fonts
- Top navbar: SportPass logo left, nav links center, AR/EN toggle + Login button right
- Sticky navbar with subtle shadow on scroll
- All cards: white background, 8px border radius, soft shadow, green hover border
- Buttons: solid green primary, gold secondary, outlined ghost variant
- Hero section: full-width, dark green background, white text, two CTA buttons,
  subtle geometric pattern overlay (SVG, Saudi-inspired)
- Responsive: mobile-first, 3-col → 2-col → 1-col grid breakpoints
